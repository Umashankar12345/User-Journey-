# Landing Copy

All hero copy, CTAs, and marketing messaging for Credex Audit Tool.

---

## Hero Section

### Headline (≤10 words)
**"See your hidden AI tool savings in 2 minutes"**

*Why this works*:
- Specific benefit (savings) + speed (2 minutes)
- Addresses exact pain (wasted spend)
- No jargon; founder-friendly

**Alternatives tested**:
- "Audit your AI spend" (too generic)
- "Save thousands on Claude, ChatGPT, Copilot" (lists features, not benefit)
- "Stop overspending on AI tools" (negative framing)

### Subheadline (≤25 words)
**"Find out if you're overspending on Claude, ChatGPT, or GitHub Copilot. Free audit, no signup required."**

*Why this works*:
- Specific tools (builds credibility)
- Free + no signup (removes friction)
- Benefit-driven (overspending resonates)

## Call-to-Action (CTA)

### Primary CTA
**"Get My Audit Results"** (on button)

*Why*:
- Action-oriented
- Possessive ("my") increases ownership
- Result-focused (not "submit form")

### Secondary CTA (after results)
**"Get your report & consultation"**

*Why*:
- Moves users toward Credex
- Consultation = credibility

### Email CTA (in confirmation)
**"See your results here →"** (with link)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: How accurate is the audit?

**Answer**: "Very. We base all recommendations on official pricing from Claude, ChatGPT, GitHub, etc. You can verify every number. See PRICING_DATA.md for sources."

*Why this works*:
- Builds trust (transparency)
- Offers verification (defensible)
- No overselling

### Q2: Will you sell my email?

**Answer**: "No. We only use it to send your audit results and, if you save >$500/month, to offer Credex's discounted credit program. That's it. See our Privacy Policy."

*Why this works*:
- Addresses trust concern directly
- Explains next steps
- References policy

### Q3: Do I have to use Credex?

**Answer**: "Nope. If you find savings, you can switch plans directly (no Credex needed). Credex offers an extra option for discount credits, but it's entirely optional."

*Why this works*:
- Honest (no lock-in)
- Explains Credex value (discount arbitrage)
- Respects autonomy

### Q4: What if I save $0?

**Answer**: "Then we'll say so. Some people already have optimal plans. If you're optimized, we'll congratulate you instead of pushing unnecessary upgrades."

*Why this works*:
- Shows integrity
- Differentiates from pushy competitors
- Builds long-term trust

### Q5: How long does the audit take?

**Answer**: "2 minutes. Enter your tools + spend, get instant results. No form validation nonsense."

*Why this works*:
- Specific time claim
- Promises speed
- Shows UX respect

## Negative Keywords (Avoid)

- "Tool" (generic, try "audit")
- "Save money" (use "savings" instead—more sophisticated)
- "Click here" (use "Get results" or specific benefit)
- "Limited time" (too salesy for b2b)
- "Act now" (same issue)

## A/B Test Ideas

1. **Headline A**: "Save $580/month on AI tools" vs B: "See your hidden savings"
   - **Hypothesis**: B (honest) outperforms A (promise)

2. **CTA A**: "Get Results" vs B: "Analyze My Spend"
   - **Hypothesis**: A (shorter) outperforms B

3. **Social Proof A**: "100+ audits" vs B: "$580 avg savings"
   - **Hypothesis**: B (number-driven) outperforms A
